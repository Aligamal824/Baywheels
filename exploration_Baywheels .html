Preliminary Wrangling¶

Baywheels Trip: Bay Area Motivate, LLC ("Motivate") operates the Bay Wheels bicycle sharing service. Motivate is committed to supporting bicycling as an alternative transportation option. Bikes are a fun and affordable way to get around. Baywheels Trip contains two types of bikes, docked bikes, and electric bikes. The services are being offered to Subscriber (Member ) or Customer (Casual).

# calculate the duration of the trip in minutes

t1 = pd.to_datetime(baywheel_copy['started_at'])
t2 = pd.to_datetime(baywheel_copy['ended_at'])
duration = []
for i in range(len(t1)):
    duration.append(pd.Timedelta(t2.loc[i] - t1.loc[i]).seconds / 60)

# creat trip duration column
baywheel_copy['trip_duration'] = duration

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

I want to know what is the most factors that affect the duration of the trip, so I will calculate the trip duration, and I want to know what is the days and time where users take a long duration trip, so I will convert the date to the day of the week and extract the start hours from the dataset.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

Bikes type, the start and end date and time, and types of members.
